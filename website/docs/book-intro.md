# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Course

Welcome to our comprehensive course on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. This course explores how to bridge the gap between digital AI systems and the physical world through embodied intelligence. You will learn to design, simulate, and deploy humanoid robots capable of natural human interactions using ROS 2, Gazebo, and NVIDIA Isaac.

## What You'll Learn

- How to create AI systems that function in reality and comprehend physical laws
- The fundamentals of ROS 2: Nodes, Topics, and Services for robot control
- Simulation environments with Gazebo and Unity for physics simulation
- Advanced perception systems using NVIDIA Isaac
- Integration of vision, language, and action systems (VLA)
- Creating autonomous humanoid robots that can interpret voice commands

## About This Book

This book provides a comprehensive foundation for building AI systems that operate in the physical world. The future of AI extends beyond digital spaces, and this book prepares you to design, simulate, and deploy humanoid robots. As you read through the content, you'll notice an integrated chatbot that can answer questions about the book itself.

## Prerequisites

- Understanding of AI concepts
- Familiarity with Python and basic robotics concepts
- Basic knowledge of neural networks (helpful but not required)
