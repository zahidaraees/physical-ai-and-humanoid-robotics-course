## About Zahida Raees

Zahida Raees (زاھدہ رئیس راجی) is a multidisciplinary scholar, educator, programmer, and writer. Her work spans Balochi language and literature, humanoid robotics and AI education, and software development. She has authored five books, contributed to Baask.com (the first digital library of Balochi literature), and maintains GitHub repositories focused on TypeScript, Node.js, and AI projects.

Zahida's diverse expertise bridges the gap between humanities and technology, making her a unique figure in both literary and technical communities. Her commitment to education is evident through her contributions to AI learning and programming assignments that help students develop practical skills.

## Projects

- **Governorsindh_AI_45_Assignement**: TypeScript solutions for AI First Quarter Assignments
- **TS-45-Assignments**: Governor Sindh IT initiative Q1, TypeScript and Node.js assignments
- **Command-Line-Simple-Calculator-Project-00**: Calculator built with Node.js and TypeScript
- **number-guessing**: Ramazan Coding Night project using TypeScript and Inquirer
- **atm**: ATM machine application built with Node.js
- **static-resume-builder-from-core-html-css**: Resume builder using HTML and CSS

These projects demonstrate Zahida's proficiency in modern web technologies, her commitment to educational initiatives, and her ability to create practical applications that serve both learning and real-world purposes.
